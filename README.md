<h2>📈 Deep Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Despite high engagement (13.56%), only <strong>0.61% users convert</strong>, indicating post-click inefficiency</li>

<li><strong>Video ads achieve highest conversion (~6%)</strong>, outperforming image and carousel formats</li>

<li>Top audience segment delivers <strong>30% higher engagement</strong></li>

<li>Engagement peaks during <strong>2 PM – 8 PM</strong>, suggesting optimal ad scheduling window</li>

<li>Geographic performance shows strong engagement in <strong>India, US, Brazil</strong></li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>💡 Business Recommendations</h2>

<ul>
<li>Shift budget towards <strong>high-performing video and story ads</strong></li>

<li>Target <strong>18–30 female audience segment</strong> for better ROI</li>

<li>Optimize campaigns for <strong>afternoon-evening time slots</strong></li>

<li>Improve <strong>landing page experience & retargeting</strong></li>

<li>Focus on <strong>high-engagement regions</strong></li>
</ul>
