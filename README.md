<h1 align="center">Marketing Funnel & Campaign Performance Analytics (Meta Ads)</h1>

<p align="center">
<b>Client-Based Analytics Project | Marketing Intelligence | Funnel Optimization | Power BI</b>
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Project Context</h2>

<p>
Developed as part of a <b>client-based analytics project</b>, this solution focuses on analyzing Meta (Facebook & Instagram) ad performance using real-world campaign data to drive data-driven marketing decisions.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🚀 Project Highlights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Analyzed <b>52,000+ campaign interaction records</b></li>
<li>Built <b>end-to-end marketing funnel (Awareness → Engagement → Conversion)</b></li>
<li>Defined key KPIs: CTR, CPC, Conversion Rate, Engagement Rate, ROAS</li>
<li>Identified <b>conversion bottlenecks and inefficient ad spend</b></li>
<li>Designed <b>interactive Power BI dashboard</b> for decision-making</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Business Problem</h2>

<p>
Marketing teams were experiencing <b>declining conversions despite high ad spend</b>, with limited visibility into customer behavior across the campaign funnel.
</p>

<p>
There was a need to identify <b>where users were dropping off</b> and which campaigns or audience segments were underperforming.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Objective</h2>

<p>
To analyze campaign performance, identify inefficiencies across the marketing funnel, and provide actionable insights to <b>optimize targeting, engagement, and conversion rates</b>.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📂 Dataset</h2>

<ul>
<li>Campaign interaction data (Impressions, Clicks, Conversions)</li>
<li>Audience segmentation (Age, Gender, Geography)</li>
<li>Campaign spend and engagement metrics</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>⚙️ Approach</h2>

<ol>
<li><b>Data Cleaning:</b> Processed raw data using SQL & Excel</li>
<li><b>Data Transformation:</b> Structured datasets for analysis</li>
<li><b>Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):</b> Identified trends and behavioral patterns</li>
<li><b>KPI Development:</b> Calculated CTR, CPC, Conversion Rate, ROAS</li>
<li><b>Funnel Analysis:</b> Tracked user journey across campaign stages</li>
<li><b>Visualization:</b> Built Power BI dashboard for insights</li>
</ol>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Key Metrics</h2>

<ul>
<li><b>CTR (Click Through Rate):</b> Measures ad engagement effectiveness</li>
<li><b>CPC (Cost Per Click):</b> Evaluates cost efficiency</li>
<li><b>Conversion Rate:</b> Measures funnel performance</li>
<li><b>ROAS:</b> Measures return on ad spend</li>
<li><b>Engagement Rate:</b> Measures audience interaction</li>
</ul>

<h3>📌 Dashboard Overview</h3>

<ul>
<li><b>Marketing Performance Overview:</b> Tracks impressions, clicks, CTR, engagement rate, conversion rate, and budget</li>

<li><b>Audience Insights:</b> Identifies high-performing segments across age, gender, and geography</li>

<li><b>Engagement Trends:</b> Analyzes weekly and hourly engagement patterns</li>

<li><b>Geographical Analysis:</b> Highlights regional performance and audience distribution</li>

<li><b>Campaign Effectiveness:</b> Compares performance across ad types</li>

<li><b>Interactive Filters:</b> Enables dynamic analysis across campaigns and audience groups</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🔍 Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Specific audience segments showed <b>30–35% higher engagement</b></li>
<li>Major <b>conversion drop-offs between click and purchase stages</b></li>
<li>Some campaigns had <b>high CPC but low conversion efficiency</b></li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>💼 Business Impact</h2>

<ul>
<li>Enabled <b>optimization of ad targeting strategies</b></li>
<li>Improved <b>budget allocation and ROI</b></li>
<li>Identified opportunities to <b>increase conversion rates</b></li>
<li>Supported data-driven marketing decisions</li>
</ul>
