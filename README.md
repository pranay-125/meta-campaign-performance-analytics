<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project focuses on analyzing the performance of digital marketing campaigns across Facebook and Instagram.
The goal is to transform raw advertising data into meaningful insights that help businesses make better marketing decisions.
</p>

<p>
It simulates a real-world client scenario where a Data Analyst is responsible for understanding campaign performance, identifying gaps, and recommending improvements.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🧠 Business Problem</h2>

<ul>
<li>Large volumes of raw ad data with no structured analysis</li>
<li>Lack of visibility into campaign and platform performance</li>
<li>High engagement but low conversion efficiency</li>
<li>Decision-making based on assumptions instead of insights</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Business Objectives</h2>

<ul>
<li>Analyze campaign performance across Facebook and Instagram</li>
<li>Identify high-performing audiences and ad formats</li>
<li>Detect conversion drop-offs across the funnel</li>
<li>Optimize budget allocation and targeting strategies</li>
</ul>

<h2>🧩 Data Modeling (Star Schema)</h2>

<p>
The data is structured using a <strong>Star Schema</strong> to enable efficient and scalable analysis.
</p>

<ul>
<li><strong>Fact Table:</strong> ad_events (interaction-level data)</li>
<li><strong>Dimension Tables:</strong> ads, campaigns, users</li>
</ul>

<h3>🔗 Relationships</h3>

<pre>
campaigns.campaign_id → ads.campaign_id
ads.ad_id → ad_events.ad_id
users.user_id → ad_events.user_id
</pre>

<p>
This model enables multi-dimensional analysis across campaigns, audience segments, and ad creatives.
</p>

<h2>📊 Key KPIs</h2>

<table>
<tr>
<th>KPI</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Impressions</td>
<td>Total number of times ads were shown</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Clicks</td>
<td>Total number of user clicks</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Engagements</td>
<td>Clicks + Shares + Comments</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Purchases</td>
<td>Total conversions</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>CTR</td>
<td>Clicks ÷ Impressions</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Engagement Rate</td>
<td>Engagements ÷ Impressions</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Conversion Rate</td>
<td>Purchases ÷ Clicks</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Purchase Rate</td>
<td>Purchases ÷ Impressions</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Total Budget</td>
<td>Total campaign spend</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Avg Budget per Campaign</td>
<td>Budget allocation efficiency</td>
</tr>
</table>

<h2>📈 Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Strong reach and engagement with high CTR</li>
<li>Low purchase rate indicating conversion funnel drop-off</li>
<li>Best-performing audience: <strong>Females aged 18–30</strong></li>
<li>Top-performing ad formats: <strong>Video and Story ads</strong></li>
<li>Peak engagement during <strong>afternoon and evening hours</strong></li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>💡 Business Recommendations</h2>

<ul>
<li>Increase budget allocation for high-performing ad formats</li>
<li>Refine targeting based on audience behavior</li>
<li>Optimize ad scheduling for peak engagement periods</li>
<li>Improve landing pages and retargeting strategies</li>
<li>Focus on high-engagement geographic regions</li>
</ul>
