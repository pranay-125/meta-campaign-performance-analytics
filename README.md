<h1 align="center">Marketing Funnel & Campaign Performance Analytics (Meta Ads)</h1>

<p align="center">
<b>End-to-End Marketing Analytics | Funnel Analysis | KPI Optimization | Power BI Dashboard</b>
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🚀 Project Highlights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Analyzed <b>52,000+ campaign interaction records</b></li>
<li>Built <b>end-to-end marketing funnel (Awareness → Engagement → Conversion)</b></li>
<li>Defined and tracked key KPIs: CTR, CPC, Conversion Rate, ROAS</li>
<li>Identified major <b>conversion drop-offs and inefficient ad spend areas</b></li>
<li>Developed <b>interactive Power BI dashboards</b> for performance monitoring</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Business Problem</h2>

<p>
Marketing teams were experiencing <b>declining conversions despite significant ad spend</b>, with no clear visibility into where users were dropping off within the campaign funnel.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Objective</h2>

<p>
To analyze campaign performance across the marketing funnel and identify <b>bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and optimization opportunities</b> to improve conversion rates and return on ad spend.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📂 Dataset</h2>

<ul>
<li>Campaign-level interaction data (Impressions, Clicks, Conversions)</li>
<li>Audience segmentation (Age, Gender, Geography)</li>
<li>Cost and engagement metrics</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>⚙️ Approach</h2>

<ol>
<li><b>Data Cleaning:</b> Ensured consistency using SQL & Excel</li>
<li><b>Data Transformation:</b> Structured campaign and audience data</li>
<li><b>Exploratory Analysis:</b> Identified trends and engagement patterns</li>
<li><b>KPI Development:</b> Calculated CTR, CPC, Conversion Rate, ROAS</li>
<li><b>Funnel Analysis:</b> Tracked user journey across stages</li>
<li><b>Visualization:</b> Built Power BI dashboards for insights</li>
</ol>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Key Metrics</h2>

<ul>
<li><b>CTR (Click Through Rate):</b> Measures ad engagement effectiveness</li>
<li><b>CPC (Cost Per Click):</b> Evaluates cost efficiency</li>
<li><b>Conversion Rate:</b> Measures funnel performance</li>
<li><b>ROAS:</b> Evaluates return on advertising spend</li>
<li><b>Engagement Rate:</b> Measures audience interaction</li>
</ul>
